This is a work in progress this summer so that I have somewhere I can point friends and family to when they are inssitent they have an idea of what my research is about.

My goal is to make an introduction for those who aren't experts in the field in a relatively quick and intuitive way, sacrificing some amount of formalism for conceptual understanding.
